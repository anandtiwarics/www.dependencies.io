FAQs

Is billing tied to my GitHub/GitLab organization?
No. You manually group repos into a "billing account" regardless of which GitHub/GitLab organization they are a part of. The subscription applies to the billing account, and you can put any repos into that account that you choose.
Do you support GitHub Enterprise?
We do not have any kind of GitHub Enterprise integration yet. If you are interested in using this with GitHub Enterprise, please contact us.
